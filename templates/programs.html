Our Programs

  • Preschool and Early Childhood Education
  • Social and Emotional Development
  • Creative Play and Discovery
  • Basic Reading and Numeracy

Preschool and Early Childhood Education

- This program focuses on building strong learning foundations for young children through guided lessons, play-based activities, and structured daily routines. Teachers guide students in a caring environment where learning is fun, meaningful, and suited to their age.

Social and Emotional Development

- This program helps children understand and express their emotions in a healthy way. They are taught how to communicate their feelings, respect others, and build positive relationships with classmates and teachers.

Creative Play and Discovery

- Through creative play, children are given opportunities to explore their imagination and express themselves freely. Activities such as art, music, storytelling, and hands-on learning allow children to discover their interests and talents.

Basic Reading and Numeracy

- This program introduces children to early reading and math skills in a fun and engaging way. They learn letters, sounds, numbers, and simple math concepts through games, songs, and interactive activities.
