Welcome to ICH

International Childrens House (ICH) is a nurturing early childhood education center dedicated to supporting the growth and development of young learners. Our school is committed to providing a safe, caring, and engaging learning environment where every child feels valued and encouraged to explore. At ICH, we believe that early learning should be joyful and inspiring, helping children develop essential social, emotional, cognitive, and physical skills. Through play-based activities, hands-on experiences, and guided lessons, we aim to foster curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning in every child who walks through our doors. Our caring teachers work closely with families to ensure that each child receives the attention, support, and guidance they need to thrive in a positive and inclusive environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the student-teacher ratio?

- We maintain a small ratio to ensure personalized attention and guidance for each child.

How do I enroll my child?

- You can contact us via phone, email, or visit our school to complete the registration process.

What safety measures are in place?

- Our classrooms are secure, monitored, and sanitized daily to maintain a safe learning environment.

Do you offer meals or snacks?

- Yes, we provide healthy snacks and meals in compliance with dietary guidelines for children.

Can children with special needs enroll?

- Absolutely, we provide inclusive education and support for children of all abilities.

Accessibility & Safety

ICH ensures that all facilities are fully accessible, child-friendly, and safe for every student. Our classrooms, play areas, and learning spaces are designed to accommodate children of all abilities, including those with special needs. Safety measures are strictly followed throughout the school, including secure entry points, monitored activity areas, non-slip flooring, and regularly sanitized classrooms and equipment. We also provide age-appropriate learning tools and furniture to ensure comfort and reduce risks. Our trained staff are always present to supervise, guide, and respond to any emergencies, creating a supportive environment where children can confidently explore, play, and learn.

Awards & Partnerships

ICH is proud to have earned multiple certifications from the Early Childhood Education Council, recognizing our high standards of teaching, care, and child development. In addition, we actively maintain partnerships with local community organizations, educational programs, and cultural institutions to provide enriched learning experiences that extend beyond the classroom. Through these partnerships, our students benefit from field trips, workshops, guest speakers, and collaborative projects that support creativity, social responsibility, and community awareness. These awards and collaborations reflect our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement in early childhood education.
