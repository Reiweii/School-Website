Student Services

  • Guidance and Counseling:

    Our trained counselors provide personal, academic, and social guidance to help students navigate challenges, build self-confidence, and develop coping skills.

    Individual or group sessions are offered depending on student needs.

  • Health and Safety Monitoring:

    The school ensures students’ well-being through daily health checks, supervision during activities, and maintaining a secure environment in classrooms, playgrounds, and common areas.

  • Parent-Teacher Communication:

    Open and respectful communication with parents is encouraged. Updates about student progress, school events, and important announcements are shared via newsletters, emails, and scheduled meetings.

  • School Events and Programs:

    Students can participate in extracurricular activities, cultural programs, and special events that promote creativity, teamwork, leadership, and holistic learning.

  • Student Progress Monitoring:

    Teachers assess students’ academic performance, behavior, and social skills regularly. Constructive feedback and support are provided to help each child achieve personal and academic growth.

  • Special Needs Support:

    Students requiring additional assistance receive tailored support plans. Collaboration with parents and professionals ensures that every child’s learning needs are met.

  • Library and Learning Resources:

    Access to age-appropriate books, learning materials, and educational tools is provided to support reading habits, research, and independent learning.

  • Technology Assistance:

    Students are guided on proper use of educational technology, promoting digital literacy and safe online practices while enhancing learning experiences.

  • Extracurricular Clubs:

    Opportunities for sports, arts, music, and other clubs are available. These activities help students develop new skills, build friendships, and enjoy balanced growth.

  • Mentoring and Leadership Programs:

    Older students can participate in mentoring programs, helping peers and taking on leadership roles to cultivate responsibility and social awareness.
