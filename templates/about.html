About Us

International Childrens House (ICH) is an early childhood education center located in Paranaque City, Philippines. Our school provides a safe, clean, and friendly environment where young children can feel comfortable, happy, and confident while learning. We aim to create a place where children are encouraged to explore, ask questions, and enjoy their early learning experiences.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to nurture young learners with love, respect, and creativity while fostering a lifelong love of learning. Our vision is to be recognized as a leading early childhood education center, where children are empowered to grow holistically and confidently.

Educational Approach

We follow a play-based, child-centered approach that promotes curiosity, creativity, and social-emotional growth. Children are encouraged to explore, discover, and learn at their own pace in a supportive and engaging environment.

Facilities & Environment

Our classrooms are bright, safe, and well-equipped with educational toys and resources. Outdoor play areas, art corners, and learning stations provide diverse opportunities for exploration and skill development.

Teacher Expertise

Our teachers are highly trained in early childhood education and provide personalized guidance and support to nurture each child’s potential.

Values & Community

We emphasize values such as kindness, empathy, and teamwork, and actively involve parents to create a supportive learning community.
