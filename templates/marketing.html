Marketing Materials

  • School Tarpaulin:

    Large-format banners used for events, open houses, and school promotion. Designed to highlight the school’s logo, tagline, and key programs to attract prospective students and parents.

  • School Brochure:

    Comprehensive brochures providing detailed information about programs, facilities, enrollment procedures, and school culture. Distributed to parents, at fairs, and during orientation sessions.

  • Official Calling Cards:

    Professional cards featuring the school’s contact information, logo, and staff representatives. Used for formal communication, networking, and inquiries from parents or partner organizations.

  • School Banner:

    Indoor and outdoor banners used for special occasions, events, or announcements. These highlight important school messages, awards, or ongoing activities to maintain visibility and engagement.
